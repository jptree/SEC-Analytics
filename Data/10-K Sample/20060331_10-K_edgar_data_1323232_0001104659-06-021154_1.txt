PART I.   

   Item 2. Properties.   

  SLM Education Credit Funding LLC, is the depositor for
the SLM Private Credit Student Loan Trust 2005-A. The property of the Trust
consists of pools of private credit education loans to students, all funds
collected in respect thereof and monies on deposit in certain trust accounts
together with rights to receive payments under certain swap transactions.  

  On an annual basis, the SLM Private Credit Student
Loan Trust 2005-A is required to provide statements of compliance of its
servicers and administrators. The Annual Statements of Compliance for the SLM Private
Credit Student Loan Trust 2005-A are attached as Exhibit 99.1 hereto.
These statements are required by:  

                           Adminstration Agreement, dated as of April
28, 2005, among SLM Education Credit Funding LLC, as the Depositor, Sallie Mae,
Inc., as the Administrator, Sallie Mae, Inc., as the Servicer, SLM Private Credit
Student Loan Trust 2005-A, as the Trust, JPMorgan Chase Bank N.A., as the
Indenture Trustee, and Chase Bank USA, National Association, as the Trustee;
and  

the Servicing Agreement, dated as of April
28, 2005 among the Servicer, the Trust, the Administrator, the Indenture
Trustee and the Trustee.
